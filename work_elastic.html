Establishing ElasticSearch DB Ecosystem

Testing ElasticSearch over MSSQL for speed, logging, and modularity in a real-time data setting.

Overview

As a database engineering intern, I was tasked with building and testing an ElasticStack pipeline including ElasticSearch, Logstash, and Kibana. The goal was to evaluate its viability and performance in contrast to MSSQL.
